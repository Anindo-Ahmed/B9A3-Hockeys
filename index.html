A professional hockey club is a team or organization that competes in a top-tier hockey league, showcasing elite talent and skill in the sport. These clubs are part of established hockey leagues around the world, such as the National Hockey League (NHL) in North America, the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) in Russia, or the Swedish Hockey League (SHL) in Sweden.
